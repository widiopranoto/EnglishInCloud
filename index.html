English in Cloud

Welcome!

English on Cloud is a way to make the life of English teachers easier. Instead of bringing tons of paper to classrooms, you can just bring an android mobile phone which is connected to internet and install this application.

How to use this application?
- To see your schedule, you can click on the schedule's icon
- To write your journal, you can click on the journal's icon
- To check and record attendant list of your students, you can click on the attendant list's icon
- To see and record students' grades, you can click on the grades' icon
- To see syllabus, lesson plan, quiz, etc, you can clik on teacher's briefcase's icon
